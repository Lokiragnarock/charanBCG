# The FPO CEO — "Prakash" 👨‍💼
> Case-user persona. The capability ceiling of the recommendation. If Prakash can't run it, it's not a recommendation, it's a fantasy.

## Profile — sourced (pulled 2026-07-10)
- Runs a 500–1,000 member FPO; turnover ₹36.4 lakh (1 activity) → ₹55.2 lakh (3 activities); net profit ₹1.2–2.5 lakh (~6% margin); working capital **< ₹3 lakh for the majority** — [IJEE 125-FPO study, 5 states, 2022](https://www.acspublisher.com/journals/index.php/ijee/article/view/6456)
- CEO + accountant are the hired professionals; board (directors) elected from members — [fpoindia.com](https://fpoindia.com/info_fpo)
- Documented governance failure modes: **lack of professional management skills, low financial literacy on boards, vested interests of directors, resistance to outside professionals, gender bias in board participation** — [IRJEMS review 2025](https://irjems.org/Volume-4-Issue-12/IRJEMS-V4I12P111.pdf)
- The scale-up trap: rapid FPO promotion has led to leader selection for subsidy-credentials rather than capability → **elite capture of company boards, non-transparent decisions** — [IFPRI, Odisha](https://www.ifpri.org/blog/navigating-growth-challenges-strengthening-farmer-producer-organizations-in-odisha-india/)
- Answerable to: NABARD/CBBO reporting, Producer Company statutory compliance, AND members who compare his price to the mandi's daily

## His constraints ARE the design constraints
| Constraint | Implication for our rec |
|---|---|
| <₹3 lakh working capital | Day-0 farmer payment needs external rails (pledge/e-NWR/anchor advance), not balance sheet |
| 1–3 staff | Any link needing a professional team (processing, retail) is out of reach year-1 |
| Political board | Member-selection rules and transparent pricing formulas beat discretion |
| Daily price comparison by members | The FPO price must be legible and beat-or-explain vs mandi, daily |
| Compliance load | Every new activity adds audit surface; simpler = survivable |

## What he'd say to our recommendation (use as a test)
"Who pays my farmers on Tuesday?" · "What happens to me if the buyer rejects the lot?" · "My board president grows 8 acres of onion — does this help him more than the 1-acre members?" · "Who fixes the app when it breaks?"
