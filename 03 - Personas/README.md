# 03 — Personas

Two kinds of personas live here:
1. **Case personas** — the users/buyers/stakeholders of whichever case we choose (e.g., for PS1: the diabetic uncle, the aesthetic-driven metro professional, the prescribing physician, the pharmacy channel).
2. **Working personas** — the hats the team (and AI) wear during analysis: the skeptic/adversary, the CFO, the regulator, the end-user advocate. Used to stress-test the recommendation and to generate "considered & rejected" material.
