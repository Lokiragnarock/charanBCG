## 1. Production & Cultivation Overview

- **Total Cultivation Area:** Remained almost constant at **17,000 Ha** (2015–2020).
    
- **Production Growth:** Increased at a Compound Annual Growth Rate (**CAGR) of 5.4%**.
    
- **Availability:** Banana is available throughout the year in Tamil Nadu, leading to significant price fluctuations compared to seasonal production in other states.

## 6. Infrastructure Status & Gaps

- **Tissue Culture Labs:** Zero in the cluster; requirements (approx. 2 crore plants/year) are fulfilled via 6 private nurseries sourcing from Hosur or Cluster-1.
    
- **Irrigation:** 75–80% of farmers use micro/drip irrigation (accounts for 8–10% of total cultivation cost), utilizing groundwater and river water.
    

### Infrastructure Facilities Matrix

|**Facility Type**|**Present Status**|**Core Constraints & Gaps**|

|**Packhouses**|5 small units (<1 MT capacity) in Theni|Primary processing happens on-field, limiting reliance on formal packhouses.|
|**Integrated Packhouses**|5 operational (2 private, 3 government). 1 new 50 MT/day unit under construction in Chinnamanur.|Capacity is highly inadequate during peak international trade periods.|
|**Reefer Vans**|Not currently utilized.|Needed at subsidized rates to maintain quality for the growing export sector.|

|**Ripening Chambers**|5 MIDH-assisted units (895 MT capacity) + household units.|High electricity requirements. Usage charge is INR 2/kg. Crucial for turning skin yellow.|
|**Quality Assessment**|No testing labs in the cluster.|Urgent need for domestic trade soil testing and quality check labs.|

## 7. Logistics & Marketing Channels

### Domestic Market Logistics

- **Primary Buyer:** Kerala (Cochin is the major hub with a trade value of **INR 351 Crore**).
    
- **Transport Mode:** Road (Pickups, TATA Ace/Ape for field-to-godown; Eicher/407 trucks for inter-city).
    
- **Domestic Transport Cost:** 1–1.5 INR/kg.
    
- **Transit Time:** 3–6 hours to Kerala.
    
- **Bottleneck:** Madurai-to-Bodi rail route is undergoing broad gauge conversion, halting rail transit options.
    

### Export Market Logistics

- **Destinations:** GCC/Middle East (Dubai, Saudi Arabia, Iran, Oman, UAE) via Sea. Minor volumes to Malaysia, Maldives, Singapore, South Korea, and London via Air.
    
- **Ports Used:** Tuticorin & Cochin seaports; Cochin, Trivandrum, and Chennai airports.
    
- **Export Transport Cost:** 2–3 INR/kg/km (Sea) | 2 INR/kg/km (Air).
    
- **Transit Time:** 10–14 days (Sea) | 24 hours (Air).
