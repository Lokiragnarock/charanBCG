# Round 1 — Submission Guidelines (distilled)

## What this is
The rulebook for the BCG OutPrompt Round 1 submission. No prescribed format — video, deck, PDF, Figma, **artifact/website all allowed**. Content > polish. AI use is *expected, not optional*.

## The six mandatory elements
1. **The case you chose** — which case and why.
2. **Your recommendation, and why** — a clear, specific call + reasoning.
3. **The evidence** — data & analysis; **cite every external figure**. An unsourced figure (including AI-hallucinated ones) counts AGAINST you.
4. **What you considered and rejected** — show the thinking, not just the winner.
5. **The Round 2 build idea** — what asset we'd build, how it strengthens the rec, what data it needs.
6. **Working the AI** — a few distinct moments of directing the AI: its first answer → what we did (pushed / corrected / stress-tested) → what changed.

## Round 1 is analysis, not a build
- Prototype = Round 2. Concept sketches / mock-ups / described experiences welcome in R1, but the deliverable is **thinking on the page**.
- R1 answer is not binding for R2; rounds scored independently.
